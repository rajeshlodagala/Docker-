# How To Connect Docker TO N8N...?

## STEP 1 : Download N8N  On Docker.

 + Open Docker.
   
 + Go To Image Or Container.
   
 +  Search N8N And Pull It

## STEP 4 : Create N8N Account.

 + Create your account with your email and set password.

## STEP 5 : Free License Key.

 + once you created n8n account the page will show like this and click send me a free license key.

   <img width="1920" height="1004" alt="Screenshot 2026-02-04 193150" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/71bf2d01-861b-44ad-a1f4-8451fc3c3a1a" />

   
   
 +  click it you get a key from your email .
   
## STEP 6 : Click Settings .

 + click usage and plan.
 + click Enter activation key.
 + now enter  your free license key and click active button .
